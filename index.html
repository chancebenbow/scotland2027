The Code

Rules of the Road

01 High score for side games: For Sneakies, Vegas, and Low/Agg — play out the hole to determine the high score. Never forfeit the high via ball-in-hand. If both opposing players forfeit, no high point awarded. A true triple bogey (gross or net) still beats a max triple or ball-in-hand.
02 OB & Lost Ball — Stroke and distance. Find a friend, estimate where it crossed OB or went into the gorse. Don't take advantage of the local exception. Honesty first.
03 Play the ball down. Exception: deep divot, on a root, or on a rock — very slightly move it without improving the lie or window. Lift, clean, and place all mud balls. Golf is hard enough.
04 Putt out non-gimmes. Net Stroke Play trophy depends on accurate stroke calculations. Gimmes are gimmes — but let's not have any "this putt doesn't matter" scoops when a putt matters.
